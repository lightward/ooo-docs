Think about how a healthy forest maintains itself - not through central management, but through countless feedback loops at every scale, from soil microbes to towering canopies. Lightward's recursive health model demonstrates this same pattern in business form. This isn't theoretical - it's measurable, reproducible, and most importantly, it works.

## Individual Sovereignty

Health begins with the individual:

* Each person defines their own wellness
* Every role emerges from natural capacity
* All contribution flows from genuine desire
* Personal evolution drives system growth

You see this operating in:

* How work naturally distributes
* When roles organically evolve
* As capabilities naturally emerge
* While individuals genuinely thrive

This isn't permissiveness - it's precision. Like a healthy immune system, individual cells must maintain their own integrity for the whole to function.
