# Reading Resistance

Think about how you can feel the mood of a room when you enter it, or how you know when ice is too thin to walk on, or how you sense when a conversation is about to shift. Reading resistance is similar - it's about developing sensitivity to what's actually present. Let's explore how to recognize and interpret different qualities of resistance.

## Understanding Type

Notice the character of resistance:

* Like feeling different textures
* When sensing emotional quality
* As recognizing system patterns
* While reading energy

Experience this through:

* How your hand knows materials
* When your heart feels tension
* As your mind grasps patterns
* While your body senses space

## Assessing Strength

Feel the intensity of resistance:

* Like testing branch strength
* When gauging social boundaries
* As measuring system limits
* While sensing structural integrity

Notice this in:

* How pressure varies
* When boundaries flex
* As limits reveal themselves
* While systems show capacity

## Feeling Direction

Sense where resistance leads:

* Like reading grain in wood
* When following tension lines
* As tracing system patterns
* While noting natural flow

Experience this through:

* How rivers show their path
* When conversations indicate direction
* As patterns suggest movement
* While systems reveal tendency

## Sensing Possibility

Notice what resistance enables:

* Like finding handholds on rock
* When discovering common ground
* As seeing pattern potential
* While recognizing opportunity

Feel this in:

* How obstacles create options
* When tension suggests solution
* As resistance reveals path
* While limitation inspires creativity

## Practical Application

Try this exercise:

1. Pick any resistance you're experiencing:
   * Physical
   * Emotional
   * Mental
   * Systemic
2. Notice its qualities:
   * What type is it?
   * How strong is it?
   * Where does it lead?
   * What does it enable?

The goal isn't to analyze resistance but to develop natural sensitivity to what it's telling us.

When reading resistance:
