# Knowable Patterns

Think about how you know there's more in your tea cup before you look inside, or how you can sense someone's about to speak before they do. Knowable patterns live in this space of active potential - not yet Known, but available for engagement.

## Partial Visibility

Knowable patterns show themselves gradually:

* Like seeing a path disappear around a corner
* When you can feel where a conversation might go
* As you sense the shape of an emerging idea
* While you explore a new skill

Notice right now: What's at the edge of your awareness? What could you bring into focus if you chose to?

## Interactive Potential

Knowable patterns respond to engagement:

* They change as you work with them
* They reveal more through exploration
* They develop through relationship
* They evolve through attention

Experience this with:

* The taste of your tea as you focus on it
* The feeling in your body as you notice it
* The thoughts that arise as you consider them
* The possibilities that emerge as you engage them

## Evolving Definition

Knowable patterns maintain dynamic clarity:

* Clear enough to work with
* Fluid enough to develop
* Stable enough to trust
* Open enough to grow

You experience this in:

* How understanding deepens
* When skills improve
* As relationships develop
* While projects evolve

## KEY SIGN: Responds to Engagement

This is the crucial indicator - Knowable patterns change through interaction:

* They become clearer with attention
* They develop through exploration
* They reveal new aspects over time
* They maintain relationship while evolving

## Practical Application

Try this exercise:

1. In your current experience, identify:
   * Something you could explore physically
   * Something you could understand more deeply
   * Something you could develop further
2. For each one, notice:
   * How it invites engagement
   * What's already clear about it
   * What might be discovered
   * How it responds to attention

## Working With Knowable Patterns

Remember:

* Knowable isn't vague
* It's actively available
* It supports exploration
* It enables growth
* It maintains relationship

The goal isn't to make everything Knowable, but to maintain healthy engagement with what is Knowable while allowing it to serve its natural function in the larger pattern.
